Pricing

iRisk does not charge broker fees for services rendered and our services are entirely at no cost to our clients. However, we are remunerated with a commission on every insurance business we place with insurance companies.

It must be noted that, the commission earned on premiums paid to insurance companies does not lead to an increase in the premium cost to our clients. A portion of every insurance premium paid has a service fee which clients pay regardless of whether they use a broker or not, and commissions paid to Brokers (iRisk) would be from this service fee.

Engaging iRisk Management will not lead to any increase in insurance premiums and may lead to a reduction, as iRisk will conduct a review of the clientâ€™s insurance portfolio, where possible, to ensure that they are not overpaying premiums for insurance policies and make recommendations for improving their portfolio.

    Solution: Several factors contribute to determining insurance premiums, including your age, driving record (for auto insurance), location, coverage limits, deductible amount, and the type of policy you choose. Our agents conduct thorough assessments to ensure you receive the most competitive rates without compromising on coverage quality.
